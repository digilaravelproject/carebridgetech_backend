How to Upload Images

1

Open the Upload Tool

Click the blue button above or open http://localhost:3000/upload-test.html in a new tab

2

Upload Your Image

Drag & drop your image onto the upload area, or click to browse. Supported formats: JPG, PNG, GIF, WebP, SVG (Max 5MB)

3

Copy the Generated URL

After upload completes, click the "Copy" button to copy the image URL to your clipboard

4

Paste in AdminJS

Go to Content Management ‚Üí Content Items ‚Üí Edit/Create item ‚Üí Paste the URL into the Image Url field

5

Save Your Changes

Don't forget to set Content Type to "image" and click Save!
